About Us

We're on a mission to make image conversion accessible, secure, and simple for everyone.

Our Story

Advanced Image Converter was born out of frustration with existing image conversion tools that were either too complicated, loaded with ads, or required uploading sensitive images to unknown servers.

Founded in 2023 by a team of developers and designers who believed there had to be a better way, we set out to create a tool that processes images directly in your browser - keeping your files on your device and your privacy intact.

Today, our tool is used by thousands of people daily, from professional photographers to casual users who just need to convert a profile picture.

Our Values

Privacy First

We believe powerful tools don't need to be complicated. Our interface is designed to be intuitive and easy to use for everyone.

We don't compromise on quality. Our conversion algorithms are optimized to maintain the highest possible image quality while reducing file size.

Innovation

Our Technology

Advanced Image Converter is built using cutting-edge web technologies that allow us to process images directly in your browser:

  • Browser-based Processing: We use the latest Web APIs to handle image conversion entirely in your browser, ensuring your files never leave your device.
  • Modern JavaScript Framework: Built with Next.js and React, our application delivers a fast, responsive user experience.
  • Canvas API: We leverage the HTML5 Canvas API for high-quality image manipulation and conversion.
  • WebAssembly: For more complex operations, we use WebAssembly to achieve near-native performance in the browser.
  • Progressive Web App: Our tool works offline and can be installed on your device for quick access.
